PennMed Video Bookmarklet

About

This is a bookmarklet that you can use with the Virtual Curriculum system. It lets you watch the lectures without using Silverlight, so you don't have to install any plugins and you can watch at 1.5x or 2x speed. Works on Windows, Mac, and Linux.

How to Use

Drag the link below to your bookmark bar. When you open a video in VC2000, click the bookmark.
